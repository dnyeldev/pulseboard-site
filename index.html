Frequently asked

Short answers. No marketing fluff.

Is PulseBoard free to use?

Yes. PulseBoard is free to download and free to use. Most widgets and features are fully unlocked on the free tier. PulseBoard Pro unlocks unlimited widgets, unlimited AI chat, and live TV for ₱149 per month or ₱999 per year.

What data does PulseBoard show?

Real-time PAGASA weather, Philippine fuel prices, PHIVOLCS earthquake activity, NLEX/SLEX toll fees, MMDA number coding, PSEi market data, Philippine airport flight tracking, live TV streams, breaking news, and an AI chat assistant — all curated specifically for the Philippines.

Does PulseBoard have ads or trackers?

No. PulseBoard is privacy-first with zero ads and zero third-party trackers. We monetize only through PulseBoard Pro subscriptions.

Which platforms support PulseBoard?

PulseBoard runs on iOS 16.0 and newer and on Android 8.0 and newer. Download from the App Store or Google Play.

How is PulseBoard different from a news app?

Traditional news apps push articles. PulseBoard is anti-social-media and anti-bloat — every widget answers a real question in two seconds, like "Will it rain today?" or "Are prices going up this week?" It's built for glanceable intelligence, not doomscrolling.
